Your doubts are answered here

What is ProtocolApp?

ProtocolApp is a protocol database specifically built for Indian labs, featuring India-specific reagents, realistic substitutes, and protocols designed for equipment commonly used in Indian laboratories.

How do I search for protocols?

Simply use the search bar at the top of the page. Type keywords related to your protocol, and you'll find relevant protocols from our database.

Can I upload my own protocols?

Yes! Use the upload section to share your protocols with the community. Make sure to follow the standardized protocol format for consistency.

Are the reagents India-specific?

Absolutely! All protocols are built around Indian suppliers and include realistic substitutes that are readily available in the Indian market.

How does ProtocolApp handle reagent quality variability?

Our protocols account for batch-to-batch and vendor-level reagent differences, ensuring reproducibility even with quality variations common in the Indian market.
